Get to know us

It isn't just about the money, it's about creating a community that promotes and believes in financial success for everyone.

Money Watchers Inc. is a Denver-based startup founded by six like-minded college students who want to achieve financial greatness. The idea started with a conversation between the current team about how we could make our mark in the world of stock markets, and leverage the information available to create something fundamentally useful for traders. Not only will you enjoy the service we provide, but you'll also obtain the ability to learn from individuals all around the world who can provide insight on the topics you care about. As we aim to be the forerunner in the stock market blogging community, we hope you'll take the leap to help us build the best community, connections, and memories alike.
